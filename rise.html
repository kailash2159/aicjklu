A NITI Aayog Initiative

Powering
Rural India

RISE (Rural India Solution for Energy) is a dedicated incubation program supporting innovations in sustainable and clean energy ecosystems that empower rural communities.

Apply the Program

Renewable Energy

Fostering startups building robust solar, wind, and bio-energy technologies specifically designed for rural landscapes.

Sustainable Growth

Supporting green technologies that empower agriculture practices and reduce the carbon footprint in rural territories.

Social Inclusion

Empowering microentrepreneurs and creating jobs by deploying decentralized energy grids in off-grid villages.

Incubation Offerings

Seed Funding

Access to government funds, initial capital, and VC networks for scaling rural operations.

Lab Access

Prototyping facilities and hardware labs provided in partnership with JKLU.

Market Access

Direct connections with NGOs, village panchayats, and rural distributors.

Expert Mentoring

Guidance from industry leaders operating in the renewable and clean tech space.
