Top-rated AIM Incubator

From Idea to Successful Venture

This is a 9-month intensive incubation program designed to help entrepreneurs materialize their ideas into successful ventures. It is open to individuals and groups at the prototype & scaling stage.

The program provides training to work on ideas, business plan development, business mentoring, product development support, technical support, and access to networking events.

Apply Now

Program Benefits

Business Plan Development

Work with expert mentors to sharpen your business model, value proposition, and go-to-market strategy.

Product Development

Access 33+ state-of-the-art labs in design, engineering, and computing to build and iterate your product.

Funding Access

Grants up to ₹20 Lakh for prototype-stage startups and investment support up to ₹50 Lakh.

Mentor Matching

Connect with 43+ domain experts through a structured mentor matching program with weekly check-ins.

Networking Events

Gain access to national and international startup ecosystems, investor networks, and industry partners.

Auxiliary Services

Legal, accounting, and other critical business support services to help you focus on building your startup.

Eligibility & Selection

Eligibility Criteria

Entrepreneurs and individuals working on innovative products, services, and processes. Applicants should be at the prototype or scaling stage.

Sectors: Energy, Eldercare, Sustainability, Fintech, Agritech, Ed-tech, EVs & more.

Selection Process

1

Application evaluation by AIC committee.

2

In-person/Online discussion for shortlisted startups.

3

Recommendation to Director, AIC for approval.

4

MoU signing and registration with AIC-JKLU.

Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) is an initiative of the Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog to promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship. The AIC serves as a platform for nurturing innovative ideas, promoting entrepreneurship, supporting innovators, and mentoring youth in general and women in particular.
